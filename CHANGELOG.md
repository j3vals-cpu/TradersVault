## v2.6.0 — cTrader Copy Trading

**New Feature: Native Copy Trader**
- Copy trades between your cTrader accounts in real-time
- Pick a master account, all others become followers
- Lot multiplier (0.1x – 100x) scales every copied trade
- FLATTEN ALL — emergency close all positions on every account
- Per-follower enable/disable and individual flatten
- Live activity log showing every copy action
- Auto-reconnect on connection drop

Open the **Copier** panel to get started.
